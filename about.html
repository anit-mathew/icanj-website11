Our History

25 Years of Faithful Ministry

Indian Christian Assembly of New Jersey (ICANJ) was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to serve the Indian-American Christian community in New Jersey with authentic worship, biblical teaching, and genuine community.

What began as a small gathering of families has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational congregation. Over 25 years, we've seen lives transformed, families restored, and generations stepping into their God-given destinies.

Today, ICANJ continues to be a spiritual home for hundreds of believers — a place where culture and faith intersect, and where everyone is known by name.

Theology

What We Believe

Our faith rooted in Scripture and the historic Christian tradition.

01

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — our final authority for all matters of faith and life.

02

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing as three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

03

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone — His atoning death, burial, and resurrection.

04

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit — empowering believers for life, worship, and witness.

05

The Church

We believe the local church is God's primary vehicle for discipleship, community, and mission in the world.

06

The Return of Christ

We believe in the literal, physical return of Jesus Christ — and we live with that blessed hope shaping our priorities.
